Leo It's a product designed for young artists who
have limited space to pursue their passion.
Modular and divided into two parts, it allows
for the grouping and organization of all
materials and tools needed for the activity.

the brief was to design a product that enables
individuals with a passion for painting,
and who have limited domestic space,
to easily pursue their hobby.
Tuo Titolo

The desk's large surface would take up a lot of space
if kept open all the time.
So, we decided to use the vertical space by creating
a structure that allows the desk surface to move
from a horizontal to a vertical position.
This way, we gain a lot of space when it's closed.
