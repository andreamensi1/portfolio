Rebit It's a home rehabilitation system that helps
restore and enhance muscles functions
of hands and wrists through the use
of interactive serious games.

The project originated from a collaboration
at Niguardia Hospital in the municipality of Milan.
The proposal was to identify a specific area
in the field of rehabilitation,
with the aim of modernizing or opening new horizons for rehabilitation equipment.
Tuo Titolo

The system consists of two handheld
controllers capable of sensing and
monitoring the pressure exerted by
individual fingers of the hand and their
movements in space.
Through specialized software, patients
will be prompted to perform targeted
actions to generate precise efforts on
specific areas of the hand.

One of the constraints for my product was accessibility.
The system had to be small, cost-effective, and portable.
The choice to draw inspiration from the world of gaming was a direct consequence of these constraints imposed earlier.
Once the product was created, I wanted to design a simple packaging to accommodate everything needed in the smallest space possible.
The user can easily receive the product in their home and transport it wherever necessary.
Rebit consists of software capable of capturing
inputs and displaying them on the screen,
interacting with rehabilitative games designed
to promote the restoration of neuromuscular functions.
The development of a virtual simulation highlights
the capabilities and mobilities that Rebit can assist patients in recovering.
