Automakers are increasingly transitioning
to vehicle electrification
continuously exploring innovations
to enhance mileage efficiency.
One intriguing technology
in this realm is 'aereowheels,'
initially introduced by Tesla.
These wheels mark a significant shift in design,
Ensuring that nothing is left to chance.

From the research conducted,
aerodynamic wheel rims and covers are
often made from exotic and expensive
materials such as carbon fiber or
CNC-machined aluminum.
Our project aimed to demonstrate that
even with less noble materials like
polymers, optimal results could be
achieved to enhance performance while
maintaining an attractive and appealing
design.
